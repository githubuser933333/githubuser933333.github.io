Rides

We've got several rides for everyone, but, just for you, we'll show ya a sneak peak of a few.

Children's Rides

Planky's Penguin Slide

Join Planky, the playful penguin, on this exhilarating slide! Children can climb to the top of a snowy mountain and zoom down on colorful sleds. With gentle twists and turns, this ride is perfect for young adventurers looking for a fun and safe way to experience the joy of winter.

Mr. Garn's Giant Adventure

Hop aboard Mr. Garn's friendly ogre train for a delightful journey through the enchanted forest! This slow-moving ride features whimsical scenery, friendly creatures, and the occasional surprise from Mr. Garn himself. Kids will love waving at the characters as they explore the magical surroundings.

Flags' Firefly Flight

Soar high with Flags, the majestic phoenix, on this enchanting flying ride! Children can hop into colorful bird-shaped cars and take to the skies, gliding above the park as they admire the beautiful views. As they circle around, Flags will guide them with his fiery glow, making every flight a memorable experience.

Planky's Snowball Toss

Join Planky for a fun game of snowball toss! This interactive ride lets kids launch soft, plush snowballs at targets while riding on a spinning platform. With laughter and friendly competition, children will enjoy testing their aim while having a blast in the winter wonderland.

Mr. Garn's Treasure Hunt

Embark on a treasure-seeking adventure with Mr. Garn! On this gentle boat ride, kids will navigate through a whimsical lagoon filled with hidden treasures and friendly creatures. Along the way, they'll hear stories from Mr. Garn and learn about the magic of friendship and kindness.

Flags' Colorful Carousel

Take a spin on Flags' vibrant carousel, featuring beautifully painted phoenixes and other enchanting creatures! With gentle music and colorful lights, this classic ride offers a magical experience for little ones as they enjoy the rhythm of the carousel while riding their favorite character.

Thriller Rides

The Sky Plunge

Prepare for an exhilarating free-fall experience! The Sky Plunge towers at a staggering 350 feet, offering riders a breathtaking view of the park before they plummet at speeds reaching up to 70 MPH. The heart-stopping drop is followed by a series of twists and turns, ensuring an unforgettable thrill for those brave enough to take the leap!

Twisted Tornado

Experience the rush of the Twisted Tornado, a high-speed roller coaster that spirals and loops at breathtaking heights of 120 feet. With speeds reaching up to 60 MPH, riders will twist through corkscrews and sudden drops, creating an adrenaline-pumping experience that will leave them breathless. Hold on tight as you navigate this whirlwind of excitement!

Fury Falls

Dive into adventure on Fury Falls, a massive water ride that propels riders up to 50 MPH! Ascend to a breathtaking height of 200 feet before plunging down a steep slide into a splash pool below. The combination of speed and height provides a thrilling experience, making it a must-ride for those seeking a watery thrill!

The Vertical Vortex

Get ready for the ultimate vertical drop on The Vertical Vortex! Standing at 300 feet, this ride launches guests straight up before sending them spiraling down at speeds of up to 65 MPH. With a series of heart-pounding drops and spins, riders will experience a rush like no other as they race through the air!

High Voltage Coaster

Feel the electricity on the High Voltage Coaster, a high-speed roller coaster that reaches heights of 250 feet! With rapid accelerations pushing riders to 65 MPH, this coaster features sharp turns and breathtaking drops that will keep you on the edge of your seat. Perfect for thrill-seekers looking for an electrifying adventure!

Riptide Rush

Ride the waves of excitement on Riptide Rush, a high-speed roller coaster that combines sharp drops with intense twists at heights of 150 feet. Reaching speeds of 60 MPH, this ride takes you through a series of heart-racing loops and hills, providing the ultimate thrill for those ready to conquer the Riptide!

Adrenaline Addict's Rides

Sky Demon

An ungodly ride with a top speed of 120 MPH, over 700 feet tall, and it all has loops, turns, upside-down sections, and more! You dare to ride it? Sorry, kid, you’ve gotta be at least 64 inches or taller. And, you’ve gotta be 13+. It’s for your safety.

The Abyss

Dive into the depths of excitement with The Abyss! Standing at an incredible 650 feet, this ride launches riders straight down at a heart-racing speed of 100 MPH. With a series of steep drops and sudden turns, it offers an exhilarating experience that will leave even the most seasoned thrill-seekers breathless. Minimum height: 54 inches; age: 12+.

Tornado Fury

Buckle up for Tornado Fury, a monstrous coaster that reaches a jaw-dropping height of 600 feet! This ride accelerates to 90 MPH as it spirals through loops and corkscrews, simulating the sensation of being caught in a wild tornado. With its high-speed twists and thrilling drops, it's a must-ride for adrenaline addicts. Minimum height: 56 inches; age: 13+.

The Vertical Rush

Get ready for a straight-up thrill on The Vertical Rush! This ride soars to an astounding height of 500 feet before a free-fall drop that reaches speeds of 110 MPH. Riders will experience weightlessness as they plunge down, followed by a series of rapid ascents and descents that will keep their hearts racing. Minimum height: 60 inches; age: 14+.

Gravity's Edge

Experience the ultimate test of courage on Gravity's Edge! Standing tall at 550 feet, this ride features a launch that propels riders to speeds of 115 MPH in just seconds. With intense twists and a zero-gravity roll, it's designed for those who crave extreme thrills. Minimum height: 58 inches; age: 13+.

The Firestorm

Feel the heat on The Firestorm, a high-speed coaster that rises to 250 feet and reaches speeds of 70 MPH. This ride features unexpected drops, fierce turns, and fiery special effects that will ignite the thrill-seeking spirit in every rider. Minimum height: 52 inches; age: 12+.

Park Guidelines

  • Height Requirements: All rides have specific height requirements. Please measure before queuing to ensure eligibility.
  • Age Restrictions: Certain rides are restricted by age. Check the signage for age limits.
  • Safety Restraints: Always fasten your safety restraints securely before the ride begins.
  • Follow Instructions: Listen to the ride operators and follow all posted instructions for a safe experience.
  • Stay Seated: Remain seated and keep your hands and feet inside the ride at all times.
  • No Loose Items: Secure all loose items, including hats, bags, and cameras, before boarding rides.
  • No Food or Drink: Eating or drinking on rides is prohibited to prevent accidents and spills.
  • Follow Emergency Procedures: In case of an emergency, follow the ride operator’s instructions promptly.
  • Health Considerations: Guests with certain health conditions should consult with staff before riding.
  • No Intoxication: Guests under the influence of alcohol or drugs may be denied access to rides.
  • Designated Eating Areas: Please consume food and beverages in designated areas only.
  • No Outside Food: Outside food and beverages are not permitted in the park.
  • Trash Disposal: Dispose of food wrappers and waste in designated trash bins.
  • Dietary Restrictions: Inform staff of any dietary restrictions when ordering food.
  • Allergen Awareness: Be aware of potential allergens in foods, and ask staff for ingredient information if needed.
  • Food Handling: Do not handle food or drinks while on rides.
  • Children Supervision: Children should be supervised when eating to avoid choking hazards.
  • Hydration: Stay hydrated, especially on hot days. Water stations are available throughout the park.
  • Food Safety: Ensure food is cooked thoroughly and served at safe temperatures.
  • Object Restrictions: No loose objects, including phones and cameras, are allowed on rides.
  • Personal Items: Leave personal items in lockers or with non-riders before boarding rides.
  • Weather Conditions: Some rides may close due to weather conditions. Check for updates.
  • Ride Maintenance: Rides may close for maintenance. Please be patient and check for information.
  • Ride Duration: Be aware of the expected duration of rides and plan accordingly.
  • Respect Others: Be courteous and respectful to all guests and staff.
  • No Running: Running within the park is prohibited to ensure safety.
  • Language: Use appropriate language and behavior at all times.
  • Photography: Respect the privacy of other guests when taking photographs.
  • Lost Items: Report lost items to guest services immediately.
  • Park Hours: Be aware of park hours and plan your visit accordingly.
  • Emergency Exits: Familiarize yourself with emergency exits and procedures posted throughout the park.
  • First Aid: First aid stations are available for any medical needs. Locate them upon arrival.
  • Report Incidents: Report any accidents or incidents to park staff immediately.
  • Evacuation Procedures: Follow staff instructions during an evacuation or emergency situation.
  • Enjoy Responsibly: Enjoy your visit responsibly and be mindful of the park rules for a safe and enjoyable experience.

Restaurant Guidelines

  • Menu Display: All menu items and prices are displayed prominently. Please check before ordering.
  • Food Allergies: Inform staff of any food allergies or dietary restrictions when ordering.
  • Cashless Transactions: We encourage cashless payments for a quicker and safer transaction experience.
  • Dining Area Etiquette: Please keep dining areas clean. Dispose of trash in designated bins.
  • Food Consumption: Food and beverages must be consumed in designated seating areas only.
  • Customer Service: Be courteous to staff and fellow guests. Respectful behavior is expected at all times.
  • Wait Times: Be patient during peak hours as wait times may vary. Staff is working hard to serve everyone.
  • Self-Service Areas: In self-service areas, please take only what you can consume to minimize waste.
  • Children Supervision: Children must be supervised at all times, especially in dining areas.
  • Sharing Food: Sharing food with others in line is discouraged to maintain order and speed of service.
  • Reservations: Some restaurants may require reservations during busy periods. Check in advance.
  • Takeout Options: Takeout is available at many locations. Please ask staff for details.
  • Special Offers: Look out for daily specials and promotions. Ask staff for details.
  • Feedback: We welcome your feedback! Let us know how we can improve your dining experience.
  • Health and Safety: All food is prepared in accordance with health and safety regulations to ensure your well-being.
